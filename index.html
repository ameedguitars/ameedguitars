About

Hi, I'm Ameed, and welcome to Ameed Custom Guitars - your premier destination for professional guitar services in Jordan. As a skilled luthier, I specialize in bringing out the best in your instrument, whether that's through precise setups, meticulous repairs, custom builds tailored to your vision, or careful restoration of treasured instruments. My expertise spans the complete spectrum of guitar work:

  • Custom Builds: Handcrafted guitars that reflect your unique musical identity

  • Professional Setups: Precision adjustments for optimal playability and tone

  • Expert Repairs: Careful restoration of damaged instruments

  • Vintage Restoration: Bringing classic guitars back to their former glory

I'm proud to partner with Jordan's leading music institutions, including Strings & Things and Rock On Festival, supporting the region's thriving music scene. Through these collaborations, I've had the privilege of working with numerous local artists and bands, helping shape the sound of Jordan's vibrant musical landscape. Whether you're looking to breathe new life into your current guitar or create your dream instrument from scratch, I combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to deliver exceptional results. Every instrument that passes through my workshop receives meticulous attention to detail, ensuring it performs at its absolute best.
